## 🟢 Task 5: Cybercrime Case Study (UPI Fraud)

UPI fraud is a common cybercrime in India where attackers trick users into sharing OTPs or approving fake payment requests. It usually happens through phishing links, fake calls, or malicious apps. Common targets include students and elderly users who may not be fully aware of online threats.

Consequences include financial loss and data theft. Victims may also face stress and privacy risks.

### Prevention Tips:
- Use official apps only  
- Never share OTP  
- Avoid unknown links  
- Verify payment requests  
- Use secure networks  
- Enable alerts  
- Report fraud immediately  
- Use cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
